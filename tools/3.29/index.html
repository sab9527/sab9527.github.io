Allflame Crafting全焰製作可提高通貨的使用效率。將物品與可對該物品使用的通貨放入製作視窗。支付亡者硫磺後，可執行該通貨原本會對物品進行的製作，但會產生多個幽靈複本，每個複本都是對原物品執行該製作步驟後的結果。全焰製作不能用於腐化物品，也不能用來腐化物品。你可選擇一個幽靈複本保留。部分製作步驟會施加無形度，限制同一物品可進行的全焰製作總量。Allflame crafting is a powerful tool that can be used to magnify the efficiency of your currency. Place an item and a currency that is valid to apply that item into the crafting window. Assuming you can pay the cost of [DeadMansSulphur|Dead Man's Sulphur], you will be allowed to perform the craft which the placed currency would usually do to that item. However, instead of just one outcome you will instead create multiple [GhostlyCopy|Ghostly Copies], each of which is the result of having performed the crafting step on the original item. Allflame crafting cannot be performed on corrupted items, nor can it be used to corrupt things. You can then choose one of these [GhostlyCopy|Ghostly Copies] to keep for yourself, effectively multiplying your currency use by the number of Copies created. Certain crafting steps will apply [Intangibility] however, which can limit the total Allflame crafting that can be done on a single item.

To transform an Enshrouded item place it in a Crystal of Permutation within the Domain of Timeless Conflict. Killing monsters in the Domain will charge the Enshrouded Item. When the Enshrouded item is fully charged you can interact with the Crystal to drop the new item.
